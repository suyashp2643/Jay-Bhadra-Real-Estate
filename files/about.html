Our Story

From One Dream to
500+ Homes

Jay Bhadra Builders was founded in 2010 by Mr. Santosh Ramesh Fatangare with a simple but powerful vision — to provide quality homes at honest prices to the hardworking families of Sangamner and the Ahmednagar district.

Starting with a small team and a single residential project, the company quickly earned a reputation for transparency, timely delivery, and uncompromising construction quality. Today, with 27+ completed projects and 500+ happy families, Jay Bhadra Builders stands as one of the most recognized names in Sangamner's real estate landscape.

Our Purpose

Mission & Vision

🎯

Our Mission

To make quality housing accessible to every family in Sangamner through transparent practices, honest pricing, and construction that stands the test of time.

  • Deliver every project on time, every time
  • Use only certified, durable construction materials
  • Maintain complete financial transparency with buyers
  • Provide 3 years of post-possession maintenance
🌟

Our Vision

To be the most trusted real estate developer in Ahmednagar district — recognized not just for the homes we build, but for the relationships we nurture and the communities we create.

  • Expand to 5+ cities in Maharashtra by 2030
  • Complete 100+ projects in the next decade
  • Develop sustainable, eco-friendly housing communities
  • Create landmark commercial mixed-use developments

Our Journey

Milestones & Achievements

2010
🏗️ Jay Bhadra Builders Established
Mr. Santosh Ramesh Fatangare registers the company and launches the first residential project with a team of 5 dedicated professionals.
2013
🏘️ First Major Project Delivered
Successfully handed over the first 20-unit residential complex on time, earning glowing testimonials and repeat business.
2016
📈 10 Projects Milestone
Expanded the team to include dedicated architects, site engineers, and a customer care division. First commercial project launched.
2019
⭐ 200+ Families Milestone
Introduced the industry-first 3-Year Maintenance Support program for all completed projects, setting a new benchmark in Sangamner real estate.
2022
🏆 500+ Families · RERA Compliance
Completed Golden City Residency — our largest project with 60+ units. All new projects fully RERA-registered.
2024
🚀 Premium & Commercial Expansion
Launched Jay Bhadra Heights and Commercial Hub simultaneously. Announced Green Valley Bungalows — first premium bungalow township project.
